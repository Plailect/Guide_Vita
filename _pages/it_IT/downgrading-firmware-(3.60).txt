### Lettura necessaria

We will now downgrade your device to firmware version 3.60 as it is the best supported firmware version which supports the Ensō boot-time exploit.

Ensō è un CFW per la PS Vita (TV) che ti permette un accesso agli homebrew più comodo, facendo avviare automaticamente un exploit ad ogni avvio della console.

It is more convenient than h-encore and Trinity as it does not require you to trigger an exploit on your device after every reboot manually.

While it is possible to run homebrew on your current firmware version using either h-encore, Trinity or Ensō, downgrading to firmware version 3.60 will provide you with the greatest homebrew compatibility.

Since the release of the reF00D system plugin, there are very few (if any) downsides to running firmware version 3.60 as this plugin allows you to install and play games which would normally require a newer firmware version.

Unfortunately, a very small number of devices will be unable to downgrade to 3.60 due to their "factory firmware" version. Further instructions will be presented when relevant for users who encounter this situation.

Ensure your device has a battery charge of 50% or greater before proceeding.

#### Sezione I - Preparazione

1. Avvia VitaShell
1. Premi (Start) per aprire le impostazioni di VitaShell
1. Premi (Croce) su "SELECT button" per cambiare la modalità a "FTP"
  + In alternativa, puoi lasciare l'impostazione a "USB" e trasferire tutti i file tramite un cavo USB
  + Nota che la modalità di trasferimento USB ti farà cominciare nella partizione ux0
  + Inoltre alcuni file saranno nascosti in modalità USB su Windows; per vedere questi file dovrai abilitare "Mostra file, cartelle e dischi nascosti" e disabilitare "Nascondi i file protetti del sistema operativo" nelle impostazioni di Esplora Risorse
1. Premi (Cerchio) per chiudere le impostazioni di VitaShell
1. Premi (Select) per abilitare l'accesso FTP sulla tua console
1. Apri il tuo client FTP sul tuo computer
1. Immetti l'indirizzo IP e la porta visualizzati sulla console
1. Utilizzando il tuo client FTP, entra in `ux0:`-> `data/`
1. Transfer `modoru.vpk` to the `data` folder
1. Transfer `PSP2UPDAT.PUP` to the `data` folder
1. Premi (Cerchio) sulla tua console per chiudere la connessione FTP

#### Section II - Installing modoru

1. Utilizzando la tua console, entra in `ux0:`-> `data/`
1. Press (Cross) on `modoru.vpk` to install it
1. Premi (Croce) per confermare l'installazione
1. Premi (Croce) per continuare l'installazione ogni volta che vengono chieste maggiori autorizzazioni
1. Press (Triangle) to open the menu, then select "Delete" to delete `modoru.vpk`
1. Premi (Croce) per confermare l'eliminazione
1. Press (Triangle) on `PSP2UPDAT.PUP` to open the menu, then select "Copy"
1. Press (Cross) to dismiss the copied file dialogue box
1. Press (Circle) to return to the `ux0:` partition
1. Navigate to `app` -> `MODORU000`
1. Press (Triangle) to open then menu, then select "Paste" to paste `PSP2UPDAT.PUP` in the current folder
1. Press (Circle) to return to the `ux0:` partition
1. Press (Triangle) on `tai` to open the menu, then select "Delete" to delete the `tai` folder
1. Premi (Croce) per confermare l'eliminazione
1. Chiudi VitaShell

#### Section III - Downgrading Prep

1. Riavvia la console
1. Launch your homebrew application
      <ul><li>For 3.65 to 3.68, this will be h-encore</li>
      <ol><li>Launch the h-encore application while holding (R)</li>
      <ul><li>If prompted about trophies, continue holding (R) and select “Yes”</li></ul>
      <li>If the exploit was successful, you will now see the h-encore bootstrap menu</li>
      <ul><li>If the exploit is stuck on a white screen, simply close the application (which will cause a crash and reboot your device), then try again</li>
      <li>If it is still stuck, hold the power button down for over 30 seconds to force a reboot, then try again</li></ul></ol>
      <li>For 3.69 and above, this will be Trinity</li></ul>
1. Seleziona "Install HENkaku"
  + Questa opzione installerà l'exploit HENkaku e abiliterà l'accesso agli homebrew fino al prossimo riavvio
1. Seleziona "Exit"
1. Apri le Impostazioni
1. Apri `Impostazioni HENkaku`
1. Select "Unlink Memory Card"
1. Ritorna a Impostazioni HENkaku
1. Chiudi le impostazioni

#### Section IV - Downgrading

1. Launch the modoru application
1. Press (Cross) to confirm the downgrade to firmware version 3.60
  + If you are given the message "Error you cannot go lower than your factory firmware.", you will be unable to downgrade to firmware version 3.60
  + If you are currently on firmware version 3.65, proceed to [Installing Ensō (3.65)](installing-enso-(3.65))
  + If you are currently on a firmware version above 3.65 and the listed "Factory firmware" is 3.65 or lower, proceed to [Downgrading Firmware (3.65)](downgrading-firmware-(3.65))
  + If you are currently on a firmware version above 3.65 and the listed "Factory firmware" is greater than 3.65, proceed to [Configuring h-encore](configuring-h-encore)
  + If you are currently on a firmware above 3.69 and the listed "Factory firmware" is greater than 3.68, proceed to [Finalizing Setup](finalizing-setup)
1. Press (Cross) to accept the terms and conditions
1. Your device will begin the downgrade process
1. Wait until the process is completed, your device will reboot automatically
