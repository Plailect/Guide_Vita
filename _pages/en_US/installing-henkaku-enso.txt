### Required Reading

HENkaku Ensō launches HENkaku automatically on boot, without having to launch it through the browser. If you lose HENkaku Ensō on 3.60, you can easily reinstall it.

### What You Need

- An FTP Client
	- For Windows, I'd recommend [FileZilla](https://filezilla-project.org/) or [WinSCP](https://winscp.net/eng/download.php)
- The latest release of [HENkaku Ensō](https://github.com/henkaku/enso/releases/download/v1.0/enso.vpk)

### Instructions

1. Open MolecularShell on your Vita
1. Press (SELECT)
1. Open your FTP client on your computer
1. Type in the IP Address and Port shown on your Vita
1. Navigate to `ux0:data` on the FTP Client
1. Transfer the Ensō `.vpk` file
1. Press (O) on your Vita to close the server
1. Navigate to `ux0:data` on your Vita
1. Press (X) on the Ensō `.vpk` file to install it
	- Make sure you still have unsafe homebrew enabled
1. Exit back to the home menu and open the Ensō application
1. Press (O) to accept terms
1. Press (X) to install Ensō
	- If it says os0 modifications detected, press (X) to install the 3.60 PUP
1. When prompted, reboot

___

After your Vita reboots, you should see the HENkaku Ensō boot logo. HENkaku will now be installed upon each reboot.
{: .notice--info}

If your Vita stops booting, hold (L) on boot to disable any plugins which might be causing issues.
{: .notice--info}

You can uninstall HENkaku Ensō by opening the HENkaku Ensō installer and following the on-screen instructions to uninstall it.
{: .notice--info}

Before uninstalling HENkaku Ensō, you must disable version spoofing and unlink your memory in HENkaku settings, otherwise your memory card must be formatted to be used.
{: .notice--warning}
