### Required Reading

Adrenaline is a homebrew application that lets you run PS1 and PSP games and homebrew from your memory card.

This will use FTP (File Transfer Protocol) to transfer the files so your Vita and PC must be connected to the same network.

The method install the Adrenaline application and then install and set up the PSP XMB.

Afterwards we will install the Adrenaline plugin. Without installing the plugin, you will have to start Adrenaline twice after every reboot.

### What You Need

- A HENkaku enabled PS Vita
- An FTP Client
	- For Windows, [FileZilla](https://filezilla-project.org/){:target="_blank"} or [WinSCP](https://winscp.net/eng/download.php){:target="_blank"} are recommended
- The latest release of [Adrenaline](https://github.com/TheOfficialFloW/Adrenaline/releases/latest){:target="_blank"}
- The latest release of [PSP Homebrew Browser](/assets/files/PSPhbb_dev.vpk)

### Instructions

#### Section I - Installing Adrenaline

1. Open VitaShell or MolecularShell on your Vita
1. Press (SELECT)
1. Open your FTP client on your computer
1. Type in the IP Address and Port shown on your Vita
1. Navigate to `ux0:data` on the FTP Client
1. Transfer the Adrenaline `.vpk` file
1. Transfer the PSP Homebrew Browser `.vpk` file
1. Press (O) on your Vita to close the server
1. Navigate to `ux0:data` on your Vita
1. Press (X) on the `.vpk` files to install both of the homebrew applications you downloaded
	- Make sure you have unsafe homebrew enabled
	
#### Section II - Installing the PSP XMB

1. Open the Adrenaline application from the home menu
1. Press (X) to download the PSP 6.61 firmware
1. Adrenaline will automatically close
1. Open Adrenaline again and press **X** to install the firmware
1. Press (X) to boot the PSP XMB
1. Set up the PSP XMB
1. Hold the PS button and select **Settings > Exit PspEmu Application**

#### Section III - Installing the Adrenaline plugin

1. Open up VitaShell or MolecularShell and navigate to `ur0:tai`
1. Open `config.txt`
1. Go to `*KERNEL` and press (/\\)
1. Select "Insert Empty Line"
1. Type into the new line: `ux0:app/PSPEMUCFW/sce_module/adrenaline_kernel.skprx`
1. Reboot the device
